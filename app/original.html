About this project

Hi, my name is Jacqueline Unanue, I am a Chilean-Spanish-American visual artist, living and working in art for almost fifteen years in Philadelphia, PA, United States.

My traveling exhibition My Ancient Land will span 3 countries: USA, Chile and Spain, during 2015. This series is inspired by the music of Chilean Classical Composers and my multicultural memories.

About the Artist

Jacqueline Unanue was born in Chile, and studied design at the Universidad de Chile de Valparaíso. She traveled extensively through Chile’s Atacama Desert and Altamira caves, Spain, doing on-site research.

In addition to numerous individual and group exhibitions in Chile since 1983, she has also been exhibited in Spain, Finland, Ecuador, Argentina, and the United States—in galleries in Washington D.C., Philadelphia, Chicago, and New York.

She lives in Philadelphia PA, USA, where she is represented by the 3rd Street  Gallery.

For more information visit her website:http://www.jacquelineunanue.com/

Inspiration                                                                                                            

My Homeland Chile Memories, and Spain andUnited States experiences

Chile, the country where I was bornand where I developed my artistic career, is firmly recorded in my memory. The colors I have used suggest -in an abstract way-, my memories of the geography, the forest, the desert, the ocean, and the Chilean sky.

Those memories are meshedwith both the experiences I had in my manytravels to Spainto connect with my father’s ancestral roots and also with myexperience in the United States where I have lived for almost fifteen years and where I continue to develop an artistic vision formed bymy multi-cultural experiences.

Inspiración

Recuerdos de mi país Chile, y mis experiencias en España y Estados Unidos

Chile, el país donde nací y donde me desarrollé artísticamente, está firmemente grabado en mi memoria.Los colores que he usado, sugieren -en un modo abstracto-, mis recuerdos de la geografía, de la vegetación, del desierto, del océano, y del firmamento chilenos.

Esos recuerdos se entremezclan con las vivencias obtenidas durante mis frecuentes viajes a España para conectarme con mis raíces ancestrales paternas, así como también con la experiencia adquirida en los Estados Unidos, donde he vivido por casi quince años y donde he seguido desarrollando una visión artística surgida desde mis vivencias multiculturales.
